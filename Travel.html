Spain

Spain is a country located on the Iberian Peninsula in Europe.

Its territory also includes the Canary Islands off the coast of Africa.

Spain is the largest country in Southern Europe

Spain is the second largest country in Western Europe and the European Union

It is the fourth largest country in the European continent.

Spain has a population of 46,733,038, making it the 30th most populated country in the world.



Malta

Malta is a Southern European island country that is an archipelago in the Mediterranean Sea.

With a population of about 475,000[4] over an area of 316 km2 Malta is the world's tenth smallest and fifth most densely-populated country.

Its capital is Valletta. Valetta is the smallest national capital in the European Union by area at 0.8 km

The official languages are Maltese and English.

Maltese is officially recognised as the national language.
